Banana Pancakes

Banana pancakes are a delicious and wholesome twist on the classic pancake, made with ripe bananas for natural sweetness. These pancakes are often light, fluffy, and packed with flavor, offering a healthier option by using bananas as a sweetener. Perfect for breakfast or brunch, banana pancakes can be enjoyed with toppings like honey, maple syrup, fresh fruits, or nuts. They’re easy to whip up, requiring just a few ingredients, and are great for those looking for a gluten-free or dairy-free option when made with alternative flours or non-dairy milk

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Mash 2 ripe bananas in a bowl, then whisk in 2 eggs and ½ cup of flour until smooth.

step 2

Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat, and pour small rounds of batter.

step 3

Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown, then serve with your favorite toppings!

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
